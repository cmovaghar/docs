Near-Humans are a type of Death Painting that perfectly adapt to their new body, making them indistinguishable from ordinary humans.

You gain the following traits.

***Ability Score Increase.*** Your Strength or Dexterity score increases by 1.

***Athletic.*** You gain proficiency in Athletics.

***Fully Human.*** Your deep understanding of human behavior grants you proficiency in two Charisma-based skills of your choice.

***Peak Physique.*** Your body has reached the pinnacle of human potential. You gain one body-related feat of your choice, provided you meet its requirements.
