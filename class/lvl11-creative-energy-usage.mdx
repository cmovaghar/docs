title: 'Level 11: Creative Energy Usage'
description: ''
---

Due to your extensive use of cursed energy, you have learned more creative ways to utilize it. You gain one of the following feats:

- [Cursed Energy Tracker]()
- [Cursed Energy Concealer]()
- [Cursed Energy Slash]()
- [Cursed Energy Ray]()

If you acquire all 4 of these feats, you may select another [Cursed Energy Feat]() that you meet the requirements for.
